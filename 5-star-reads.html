My 5 star reads

Below I've listed a few of the books I've enjoyed recently.


Title Author Thoughts
The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo Taylor Jenkins-Reid Became absolutely absorbed in the Hollywood glamour, almost too scared to watch the TV adaptation incase it ruins it for me.
Friendaholic Elizabeth Day Very interesting exploration of friendships and how they evolve throughout life. I also went to see Elizabeth talk about this book at the Cheltenham Literature Festival.
I'm glad my Mom Died Jeanette McCurdy Shocking title, but a scary insight into kid/teen stars and the murky side of TV.
The Satsuma Complex Bob Mortimer Read this in one day, utterly bonkers but hilarious read.

I enjoy watching book reviews on TikTok and Youtube to discover new authors and genres, a creator I like to watch is Jack Edwards.
